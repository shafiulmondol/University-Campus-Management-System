-----Objectives and Preamble-----

Objectives:

SKST University Alumni Association plays a vital role by assisting the university in developing linkage with industries, multinational organizations as well as other firms and companies. Besides that, it assists the interns of SKST University in finding internship placement in different organizations. It also provides job opportunities to the students of SKST University.

Preamble:

We, the Alumni of the SKST University, develop mutual interest among ourselves, establish mutually beneficial relations between SKST University and its Alumni, and promote the interests of SKST University, do hereby form into an Association and adopt this constitution.
