About SKST University Scholarships

SKST University is committed to supporting meritorious but financially needy students through a variety of scholarships. Our scholarships aim to promote academic excellence, encourage talented students, and make higher education accessible to students from all walks of life.

Scholarships are awarded based on academic performance, financial need, and in some cases, program-specific eligibility. Students from different programs, districts, and special categories are encouraged to apply. Each scholarship has specific criteria, amount, and number of slots which are updated every semester.

SKST University believes that investing in students’ education strengthens the community, nurtures future leaders, and contributes to national development. We encourage eligible students to explore available scholarships and apply for the opportunities that best match their profile.
