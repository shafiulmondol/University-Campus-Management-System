-----About SKST Library-----

SKST University Central Library is Bangladesh's first private university library that is completely automated by KOHA. SKST University Central Library serves as a cornerstone of knowledge and community participation, offering a diverse range of books and services to fulfill the needs of its patrons. The SKST University Central Library has printed and online resource that assists the university's teaching and research activities. Library has a wide range of printed collections contain around 41,000 materials, with over 81,000 online e-resources. The Library features an outstanding digital library system for e-readers, with access to several online periodical databases, including over 41000 e-books and e-journals such as IEEE, LiCOB, and Research4Life in SKST University Online e-resources. SKST University Central Library is one of the largest private university libraries in Bangladesh, spanning over 18,000 square feet.

The library is organized into the following sections: Circulation, Acquisition and Processing, Archives, E-Library, Founder's Corner, Bangabandhu and Liberation War Section, Fiction Corner, Journals and Periodicals, and News Clippings. The IUBAT Library's collections include books, CDS, DVDs, journals, magazines, practicum reports, IUBAT publications, periodicals, newspapers, manuscripts, films, maps, prints, documents, microforms, cassettes, videotapes, Blu-ray Discs, e-books, audiobooks, databases, and other formats. These collections can be reached through the Online Public Access Catalogue. Approximately 2,000 users (researchers, professors, officers, and students) utilize library with barcode-printed ID cards in everyday.

-----Borrowing Guidelines for Library Users-----

Borrowing Guidelines:

1. Library users must show their valid ID card to borrow books and other library resources.
2. Textbooks, Current and Rare documents are not allowed to issue.
3. Register your ID in library OPAC for book borrowing.
4. Every book that you borrow must be returned by the due date. If you require the same book for the following semester, you can borrow it again, but it must be returned before the new semester begins.
5. All borrowed books must be returned on the due date or the borrower is to pay overdue charges at the following rates.

-----Replacement and Cost of Damaging Library Resources-----

Books and Documents: Any books or documents lost by the borrower must be replaced with a new copy of the same author(s), title, and edition. A book(s) of a single author, title, and edition cannot be substituted by a book(s) or documents of another author and title. For missing books or documents, the borrower will be liable for the current market value of the lost book as a fee to the Library including 20% service charge.

-----Rules for Library Clearance-----

Library clearance typically involves returning all borrowed materials, including books, journals, multimedia resources and any other items checked out from the library. Fines or fees must be settled, including overdue fines, replacement costs for lost or damaged items, and any other charges incurred during the borrowing period. The Softcopy and Hard Copy of practicum (with certificate) and thesis report including supervisor signature must be submitted to the library for library clearance.
